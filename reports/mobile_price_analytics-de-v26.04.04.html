Medallion Layer Configuration

  • Three-Tier Design: Each layer enforces an explicit data contract at its output boundary : no cross-layer contamination.
  • Resilience: When transformation logic changes, only Silver and Gold need reprocessing : Bronze remains immutable as the permanent reprocessing baseline.
  • Bronze (Landing Zone): Zero transformation, full fidelity, append-only : every record lands unchanged including duplicates, malformed rows, and DQ failures.
  • Silver (Trusted Data): Deduplicated, typed, null-handled, PII-masked, DQ-validated : safe for direct analytical consumption by data scientists and analysts.
  • Gold (Business-Ready): Pre-computed KPIs and group-level aggregations : eliminates runtime aggregations in dashboards, ensuring metric consistency across all consumers.
